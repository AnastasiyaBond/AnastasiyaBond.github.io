About me

In 2018 I successfully graduated the School of business and management of technologies at Belarussian State University with a degree in Business Administration. During the university I had internships and work experience in various organizations, gaining practical experience and skills in the field of marketing, analytics and web technologies. Working as a marketing analyst intern at Artox and Style-Decor companies, I gained the following skills: making reports on targeting, maintaining statistics on rubrics, traffic planning, calculation of residual cost of ad impressions, calculation of rubrics CPC and capacity for projects, editing customer pages, creation of promo blocks, competitive analysis, marketing mix of the product nomenclature. My working activity began as a media planner in Nominal-Technology, the company in digital marketing industry. At the moment I work in Datamining company in the position of marketing analyst, combining the functions of sales-manager. The scope of my functions includes: preparing analytical reports, communications with customers of marketing research, providing documentation from sending invoices to closing acts.

My area of interest also includes business analysis, and as a result I successfully completed business analysis course at the IBA Institute and improved my skills in designing information systems using UML and BPMN notation. Among my additional achievements: prize-winner (2 place) with the work "The role of marketing in the managemant of Belarusian organisations' activity " at International scientific and practical conference 2017, prizewinner (3rd) of the city olympiad in English.
